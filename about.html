About Me

Hello! I'm Harshita Mohapatra, a Computer Engineering student with a strong interest in technology and software development. I enjoy learning new concepts, building projects, and understanding how technology can be used to solve real-world problems.

As a student who has just completed first year of my Computer Engineering degree, I am sure I have alot that I still dont know about but will learn in the coming years.

My journey into programming began with learning C and C++, which helped me develop logical thinking and problem-solving skills. As I explored further, I became interested in web development and started learning HTML, CSS, Git, and GitHub. I enjoy creating projects because they allow me to apply what I learn and gain practical experience beyond classroom studies.

Apart from academics, I actively focus on self-learning through online resources, documentation, tutorials, and hands-on practice. I believe that consistency and curiosity are two of the most important qualities for growth in the field of technology.

Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in areas such as Data Structures & Algorithms, Cloud Computing, and modern development tools. I am always open to learning new technologies and exploring different domains within computer science.

One of the things I enjoy most about engineering is that there is always something new to learn. Whether it is solving a programming problem, understanding a new concept, or building a project from scratch, I find the process both challenging and rewarding.

Outside of technical learning, I enjoy participating in workshops, competitions, and activities that contribute to my overall development. I believe that growth comes not only from academics but also from experiences that improve communication, teamwork, adaptability, and confidence.

My current goal is to strengthen my technical foundation, gain practical experience through projects and internships, and continue developing the skills required to become a capable software engineer.
